Sena Repo bundled 7-Zip-zstd binaries
=====================================

This directory contains prebuilt archive extraction binaries used by the
Sena Repo client.

The binaries are built from 7-Zip-zstd:
  https://github.com/mcmilk/7-Zip-zstd

7-Zip-zstd is based on 7-Zip by Igor Pavlov and adds support for additional
compression methods such as Zstandard.

Relevant upstream projects:
  7-Zip: https://www.7-zip.org/
  7-Zip-zstd: https://github.com/mcmilk/7-Zip-zstd
  Zstandard: https://github.com/facebook/zstd
